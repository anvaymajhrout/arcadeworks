Shakespeare Timeline

1564

William Shakespeare was born in Stratford-upon-Avon.

1582

Shakespeare marries Anne Hathaway.

1585

Birth of twins, Hamnet and Judith.

1590

Shakespeare begins writing plays.

1599

The Globe Theatre is built.

1601

Shakespeare's father, John Shakespeare, dies.

1603

Queen Elizabeth I dies; James I becomes king.

1609

Shakespeare's Sonnets are published.

1613

The Globe Theatre burns down.

1616

William Shakespeare dies.
