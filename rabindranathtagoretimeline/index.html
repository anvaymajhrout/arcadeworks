Rabindranath Tagore Timeline

1861

Rabindranath Tagore is born in Calcutta, India.

1877

Tagore's first substantial poems are published.

1883

Tagore marries Mrinalini Devi.

1901

He founds the school at Shantiniketan.

1913

Tagore wins the Nobel Prize in Literature.

1915

He is knighted by the British King George V.

1919

Tagore renounces his knighthood in protest of the Jallianwala Bagh massacre.

1941

Rabindranath Tagore passes away.
